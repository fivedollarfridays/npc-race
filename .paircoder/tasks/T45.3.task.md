```
TIME TRIAL — Monza

  Lap time:  1:27.370

  EFFICIENCY BREAKDOWN
  ├── Gearbox:    0.93  ← shifts past peak torque
  ├── Cooling:    0.88  ← high drag (effort too high)

  Tip: Peak torque is 10,800-12,500 RPM. Try shifting at 11,000.
```
