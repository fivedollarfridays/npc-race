# T9.3 — Spin & Lockup Incidents

## Objective

Create `engine/incident.py` — computes per-tick probability of a car spinning or locking up based on grip demand vs available grip. High tire wear + dirty air + cold tires + aggressive driving = spin risk. A spin costs 3-5 seconds and may trigger safety car.

## Design

**Spin risk per tick:**
```
base_risk = 0.00005  (very low per tick, but adds up)
× grip_deficit_factor  (1.0 if driving within grip, up to 5.0 if demanding 50% more than available)
× dirty_air_factor     (1.0 clean, up to 1.5 in heavy dirty air)
× tire_factor          (1.0 fresh, up to 3.0 near cliff)
× cold_tire_factor     (2.0 first 2 laps after pit, 1.0 otherwise)
```

If `random() < spin_risk`: spin event!

**Spin outcome:**
- Speed drops to 20 km/h
- 3-5 second delay before resuming (90-150 ticks)
- Lose 1-3 positions
- Tire wear jumps +0.05 (flat spot from lockup)
- 20% chance triggers safety car (if in high-curvature zone)

**Lockup (less severe):**
- When braking demand exceeds brake grip
- Flat spot: tire wear += 0.02, vibration for rest of stint
- Speed penalty: -5 km/h for 30 ticks
