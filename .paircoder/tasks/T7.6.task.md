# T7.6 — Integration Gate: F1 Data Validation

## Objective

Validate Sprint 7 against real F1 2024 data. This is the most thorough integration gate yet — we compare simulation output directly against documented F1 telemetry.

## Implementation Plan

### New file: `tests/test_f1_validation.py`

Tests that compare NPC Race output to real 2024 F1 data:

**Class `TestMonzaValidation`:**
- `test_monza_fastest_lap_within_15pct` — fastest lap within 15% of real 81.4s → 69-94s
- `test_monza_top_speed_realistic` — max speed 280-370 km/h (real: 357 km/h DRS trap)
- `test_monza_min_speed_realistic` — min racing speed > 50 km/h (real: 72 km/h at chicane)
- `test_monza_dirty_air_visible` — at least 10% of frames for following cars show in_dirty_air=True

**Class `TestMonacoValidation`:**
- `test_monaco_fastest_lap_within_20pct` — fastest lap within 20% of real 74.2s → 59-89s
- `test_monaco_is_slower_than_monza` — Monaco lap > Monza lap (it's a slower circuit)

**Class `TestTireStrategyValidation`:**
- `test_soft_tire_degrades_faster_than_medium` — over 5 laps, soft wear > medium wear
- `test_tire_temp_in_f1_range` — mid-race temps between 60-120°C
- `test_compound_pace_delta` — fresh soft is faster than fresh medium (lap 2 comparison)
- `test_tire_cliff_exists` — at high wear (>0.8), grip drops sharply

**Class `TestFuelValidation`:**
- `test_fuel_consumption_per_lap` — 1.5-3.0 kg consumed per lap (track-dependent)
- `test_fuel_load_affects_pace` — car with more fuel is slower (compare early vs late stint)

**Class `TestPitStrategyValidation`:**
- `test_pit_stop_time_loss_realistic` — pit stop costs 20-25s vs not pitting
- `test_two_stop_viable` — 2-stop strategy on 10-lap race isn't catastrophically slow

**Class `TestDirtyAirValidation`:**
- `test_following_car_slower_in_corners` — car 0.5s behind loses time through corners
- `test_following_car_gains_on_straights` — car behind gains time on straights (draft)
- `test_dirty_air_creates_overtaking_dilemma` — following is NOT purely beneficial (mix of gain and loss)

**Class `TestDownforceValidation`:**
- `test_high_speed_corners_faster_with_high_aero` — car with aero=35 faster in fast corners than aero=15
- `test_low_speed_corners_similar_regardless_of_aero` — in tight hairpins, aero matters less
- `test_high_aero_slower_on_straights` — high wing_angle car slower top speed

### Calibration script update

Update `scripts/calibration_check.py` to include dirty air stats and aero grip analysis.

- Tire degradation visible over stints
- Fuel visibly consumed
