# T13.3 — Race Report

## Objective

Create `engine/race_report.py` — generates a post-race text report from race results and detected events. Identifies the decisive moment, driver of the day, and key strategy calls.

## Design

**Report structure:**
```
{TRACK} GP — RACE REPORT
Winner: {name} ({strategy description})
Decisive moment: Lap {N} — {event description}
Safety cars: {count}
Incidents: {count} spins, {count} contacts
Driver of the day: {name} ({reason})

KEY MOMENTS:
1. Lap 5 — GooseLoose passes Silky for P1
2. Lap 12 — Safety car after BrickHouse spin
3. Lap 18 — SlipStream sets fastest lap (56.4s)
```

**Driver of the day**: car that gained the most positions from start to finish.

**Decisive moment**: the overtake for P1, or the last safety car, or the fastest lap — whichever happened latest.
