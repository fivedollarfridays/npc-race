# T8.6 — Post-Race Diagnostic Mode

## Objective

When the race ends, a "FULL DIAGNOSTIC" toggle appears. It expands the telemetry strip to show the entire race timeline (not rolling window) and adds a lap time bar chart, tire strategy visualization, and sector-by-sector breakdown table. Only available for the player's car.

## New File: `viewer/js/diagnostic.js` (~180 LOC)

### Trigger

Race finishes → "DIAGNOSTIC" button appears in the status bar. Click toggles between live-replay view and diagnostic view.

### Diagnostic View Layout

The telemetry strip area expands to fill the entire bottom half of the screen (track view and telemetry panel shrink or are replaced). Shows:

**Panel 1: Lap Time Bar Chart** (~120px)
- Horizontal bar chart, one bar per lap
- Bar color = tire compound (red=soft, yellow=medium, white=hard)
- Bar length = lap time (scaled so fastest lap fills ~80% width)
- Fastest lap bar highlighted with purple border
- Pit in-laps marked with "P" label
- Y-axis: lap numbers

**Panel 2: Tire Strategy Timeline** (~60px)
- Horizontal stacked bars showing stints
- Each stint: compound color, length in laps
- Vertical marks for pit stops
- Overlay: tire wear line across the full race

**Panel 3: Sector Breakdown Table** (scrollable)
```
Lap  S1       S2       S3       Total    Compound  Notes
1    33.021   32.456   31.890   97.367   MED       warm-up
2    31.890   31.234   31.456   94.580   MED
3    31.234   31.890   31.012   94.136   MED       personal best
4    32.100   32.890   31.890   96.880   MED       dirty air S1-S2
...
18   PIT IN   ---      ---      ---      ---       → SOFT
19   34.123   31.012   30.890   96.025   SOFT      cold tires
```

Each sector colored purple/green/yellow vs personal best.
"Notes" column auto-generated: "warm-up", "personal best", "dirty air S1-S2", "cold tires", "tire cliff", "pit in", "pit out".

**Panel 4: Cumulative Gap Chart** (~80px)
- Full race timeline (all laps)
- Line chart of gap to leader over entire race
- Pit stop gaps visible as jumps
- Undercut/overcut moments labeled

### Player car restriction

The diagnostic button ONLY appears for the car that was initially selected (the player's car). If you click another car in the timing tower, the live telemetry shows their data, but the diagnostic toggle disappears. Click back to your car → diagnostic available again.
