# T7.1 — Dirty Air System

## Objective

Model aerodynamic turbulence from a leading car. In real F1, following closely through corners REDUCES grip (dirty air disrupts downforce), while following on straights provides a draft (already modeled). This creates the core F1 overtaking dilemma.

## Research Basis

- f1metrics: 1.1x tire degradation when following closely
- FormulaGPT: speed penalty when in dirty air through corners
- Real F1: cars lose up to 50% of downforce when within 1 car length in corners
- Effect diminishes with distance: negligible beyond ~1.5-2.0 seconds gap
