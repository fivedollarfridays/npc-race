# T15.1 — TV Director Camera

## Objective

Add a "director" camera mode to `viewer/js/camera.js` that auto-switches between cars based on race action. Uses narrative events (from replay JSON) to decide what to show.

## Design

**Director logic (priority order):**
1. **Overtake**: snap to overtaking car 2s before, hold 3s after (highest priority)
2. **Battle**: switch to battling pair when gap < 1.0s for 3+ seconds
3. **Pit stop**: cut to pit area for 5s when a car pits
4. **Safety car**: cut to wide view during SC
5. **Default**: follow the leader

**State:** `_directorTarget` (car name), `_directorHoldUntil` (frame number), `_directorMode` (leader/battle/overtake/pit/sc)
