# T34.4 — Strategy Throttle to 1Hz

Cache strategy decisions, call player strategy every 30 ticks instead of
every tick. Skip `build_strategy_state` on non-strategy ticks.

Compute lightweight `gap_ahead_s` from sorted positions every tick
(needed for dirty air physics).
