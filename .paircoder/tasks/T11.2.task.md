# T11.2 — ERS Model

## Objective

Create `engine/ers_model.py` — Energy Recovery System with battery deploy/harvest. Strategy chooses deploy mode; ERS affects speed and braking recovery.

## Design

**Battery:** 0-4 MJ capacity per lap. Resets each lap (F1 rules: 4MJ deploy limit per lap).

**Deploy modes:**
- `attack`: 120kW boost, drains battery fast (~12s at full deploy)
- `balanced`: 60kW boost, moderate drain (~24s)
- `harvest`: no deploy, maximizes harvest under braking

**Harvest:** Under braking, MGU-K recovers energy proportional to braking force.
- Recovery rate: `braking_force * 0.02 * dt` MJ per tick
- Capped at 2 MJ harvest per lap (F1 MGU-K limit)

**Speed effect:**
- `attack`: +8 km/h top speed bonus
- `balanced`: +4 km/h
- `harvest`: 0 (no deploy)
- Only applies when battery > 0
