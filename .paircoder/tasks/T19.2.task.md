# T19.2 — Racing Line Model

## Objective

Create `engine/racing_line.py` — computes optimal lateral position at every track point. Inside on corner entry, clip the apex, wide on exit. Straight sections use center or draft positions.

## Design

**Racing line from curvature:**
- Straight (curv < 0.005): lateral = 0.0 (center)
- Corner entry (curv increasing): lateral moves toward inside (-0.8)
- Apex (peak curvature): lateral = -1.0 (full inside)
- Corner exit (curv decreasing): lateral moves toward outside (+0.5)

The direction of the "inside" depends on whether the corner turns left or right, determined from track heading changes.

**Simple heuristic:** Use curvature sign/magnitude and rate of change to compute a smooth lateral target at each track point.
