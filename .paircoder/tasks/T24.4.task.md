# T24.4 — ERS Efficiency

Implement efficiency for ers_deploy and ers_harvest.

**ERS deploy:** efficiency = speed_gain_realized / speed_gain_if_optimal. On a straight (power-limited), deploying 120kW adds ~10 km/h — high efficiency. In a corner (traction-limited), the power exceeds grip and is wasted — low efficiency. The physics must distinguish these cases.

**ERS harvest:** efficiency = energy_recovered / max_possible_recovery. Under-harvesting wastes braking energy. Over-harvesting when battery is full or hot wastes the opportunity.

4 MJ/lap deploy limit and 2 MJ/lap harvest limit must be enforced per lap.
