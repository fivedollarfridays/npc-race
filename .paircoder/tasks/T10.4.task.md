## Seed car updates (2-3 cars)

**GooseLoose** — weather-aware compound switching:
- If `track_wetness > 0.4` and compound is dry (soft/medium/hard): request pit for intermediates
- If `track_wetness > 0.7` and compound != "wet": request pit for wets
- If `track_wetness < 0.15` and compound in ("intermediate", "wet"): switch back to mediums

**Silky** — forecast-aware:
- Check `weather_forecast` for upcoming rain
- If forecast shows wetness > 0.4 within 2 laps and on dry tires with low wear: stay out (don't waste tires)
- If forecast shows wetness > 0.4 within 2 laps and wear > 0.4: pit for intermediates early

**SlipStream** — aggressive wet strategy:
- If `track_wetness > 0.3` and on dry tires: pit immediately for intermediates (early switch)
- Aggressive: switch back to drys sooner than others when drying
