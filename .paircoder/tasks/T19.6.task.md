# T19.6 — Integration Gate: Monza Calibration

## Objective

Verify the driver model produces realistic Monza lap times and speed profiles. Target: lap time within 5 seconds of real F1 (~1:20).

## Tests: `tests/test_driver_calibration.py`

Mark with `@pytest.mark.slow`.

**Class TestMonzaCalibration:**
- `test_monza_lap_time_75_to_85` — best lap between 1:15 and 1:25
- `test_monza_top_speed` — max speed between 310-355 km/h
- `test_monza_min_corner_speed` — min speed (excluding spins) between 60-140 km/h
- `test_speed_variation` — standard deviation of speeds > 50 km/h (not stuck at one speed)
- `test_braking_zones_exist` — at least 3 sections where speed drops > 80 km/h within 30 ticks

**Class TestDriverBehavior:**
- `test_driver_brakes_before_corners` — speed profile decreases before high-curvature sections
- `test_driver_accelerates_after_corners` — speed profile increases after corner exits
- `test_all_cars_finish` — at least 4 of 5 cars finish a 2-lap race

**Class TestBackwardCompat:**
- `test_existing_cars_still_work` — all 5 seed cars run without errors
- `test_strategy_override_works` — car that sets throttle=0.5 runs slower

**Class TestArchCompliance:**
- `test_simulation_under_limits` — ≤ 400 lines, ≤ 15 functions
- `test_driver_model_under_limits` — ≤ 150 lines
- `test_speed_profile_under_limits` — ≤ 100 lines
- `test_racing_line_under_limits` — ≤ 80 lines

## Acceptance Criteria

- Monza lap time within 1:15-1:25 (real: ~1:20)
- Visible braking zones and acceleration zones
- Speed varies between ~70-340 km/h through the lap
- All seed cars work without modification
- Sprint 19 complete
