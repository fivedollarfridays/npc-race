# T30.1 — League Definitions + Validation

Create `engine/league_system.py` with tier definitions and validation.

## Tiers (based on measured sensitivity)
- F3: gearbox, cooling, strategy (3 parts, 1-lap races)
- F2: + suspension, ers_deploy, fuel_mix (6 parts, 3-5 lap races)
- F1: all 10 parts (full race distance)
- Championship: all 10 + multi-file + quality gate

## Functions
- determine_league(car) → str: infer tier from _loaded_parts count and composition
- validate_car_for_league(car, league) → LeagueResult: check parts match tier restrictions
- LeagueResult dataclass: passed, league, loaded_parts, violations

## AC
- 3 F3 parts auto-detected as F3
- 6 F2 parts auto-detected as F2
- Car with disallowed parts for tier rejected with specific feedback
