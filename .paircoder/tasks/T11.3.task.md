# T11.3 — Brake Temperature Model

## Objective

Create `engine/brake_model.py` — brake temperature rises under heavy braking, cools from airflow. Hot brakes fade, reducing braking effectiveness and affecting corner entry speed.

## Design

**Temperature model (similar to tire temp):**
- Ambient: 20°C, optimal: 300-600°C, fade above 700°C
- Heating: proportional to braking_force × speed (kinetic energy → heat)
- Cooling: proportional to speed (airflow) and temp delta from ambient

**Fade effect:**
- Below 700°C: brake_efficiency = 1.0
- 700-900°C: linear fade from 1.0 to 0.6
- Above 900°C: brake_efficiency = 0.6 (severe fade)

**Speed impact:** `effective_braking = brakes × brake_efficiency`
- Low efficiency → longer braking distances → slower corner entry
