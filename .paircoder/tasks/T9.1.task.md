# T9.1 — Collision Detection System

## Objective

Create `engine/collision.py` — detects when two cars make contact based on proximity (distance gap + lateral overlap). Returns collision events with severity. Pure functions, no simulation changes.

## Design

Two cars collide when:
- Distance gap < `CONTACT_DISTANCE` (3.0 sim units ≈ 1 car length)
- Lateral difference < `CONTACT_LATERAL` (0.25 ≈ same part of track)
- Neither car is in pit lane
- Both cars are racing (not finished)

Collision severity is probabilistic based on relative speed and angle:
- MINOR (50%): both lose 5-15 km/h, no damage
- MODERATE (30%): one car spins (3-5s time loss), minor damage
- SEVERE (15%): front wing damage, significant speed penalty
- CRITICAL (5%): DNF for one car (retirement)
