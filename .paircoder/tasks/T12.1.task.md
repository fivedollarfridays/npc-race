# T12.1 — Opponent Visibility Model

## Objective

Create `engine/visibility.py` — defines what data about opponents is visible to a car's strategy function, and builds filtered opponent info lists.

## Design

**Observable (things you can see from the cockpit):**
- name, position, speed, lateral position
- gap_ahead_s (timing board data)
- tire_compound (visible from tire color)
- tire_age_laps (announced on TV/pit board)
- pit_stops (announced)
- drs_active (visible — wing is open)
- in_spin (visible — car is spinning)
- finished (visible)

**Hidden (private telemetry only your team has):**
- tire_wear (exact degradation %)
- fuel_remaining, fuel_pct
- tire_temp
- engine_mode
- damage (exact value)
- ers_energy, ers_deploy_mode
- brake_temp
- spin_risk
- dirty_air_factor
