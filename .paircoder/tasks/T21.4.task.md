# T21.4 — Migrate Seed Cars

Rewrite all 5 seed cars (GooseLoose, BrickHouse, GlassCanon, Silky, SlipStream) using the new parts format. Each car should have a distinct engineering personality expressed through its part code:

- **GooseLoose**: Balanced parts, smart fuel management, SC-aware strategy
- **BrickHouse**: Late braker (aggressive brake_bias), heavy harvester (ers_harvest), conservative gearbox
- **GlassCanon**: Max engine_map (high torque %), aggressive fuel_mix (rich), fragile cooling
- **Silky**: Smooth suspension (stable ride height), optimized differential (corner-phase-aware), brake temp management
- **SlipStream**: Low-drag aero spec, aggressive ers_deploy on straights, tall gearbox ratios

Each car retains its old strategy() for pit decisions but gains 9 new part functions that define its engineering character.
